QUESTION FOR UNEMPLOYMENT

Q/ How does this challenges affect me?

Unemployment has likely caused financial stress, emotional strain, and a loss of routine, affecting your mental health and self-esteem. It may have led to feelings of isolation and frustration, while also hindering career growth and future financial security.

Q/ What can i change in my community?

You can help your community by organizing job fairs, skill-building workshops, and offering resume or career counseling. Encouraging local businesses to hire or mentor job seekers and creating support networks can strengthen both individuals and the community as a whole.

how does this challenge affect my community?

Unemployment can lead to increased financial strain, social isolation, and mental health challenges in your community. It may also cause higher crime rates, reduced economic activity, and a lack of opportunity, weakening the overall well-being and growth of the community.

You can create job training programs, skill-building workshops, and support networks to help people find employment and improve their skills.
